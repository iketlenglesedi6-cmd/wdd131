Soweto

About

Soweto, short for South Western Townships, is a historic and culturally rich area located south of Johannesburg. It played a central role in South Africa’s fight against apartheid and remains a powerful symbol of resilience. Home to Vilakazi Street, where Nelson Mandela and Desmond Tutu once lived, Soweto blends deep history with vibrant music, art, and daily life. Today, it stands as a place of pride, storytelling, and community spirit.
