Site Name

Safe Space Poetry

This name reflects the purpose of the site as a welcoming, healing space where people can share poetry, emotions, and personal stories without judgment.

Site Purpose

The website provides a safe, inclusive space for poetry lovers to read, share, and submit poems. It features original poems, community submissions, and allows visitors to request poems or offer feedback. The purpose is to encourage emotional expression, healing, and connection through words.

Scenarios

  • How can I submit my own poem to be shared on the website?
  • Where can I read poems that relate to healing, love, or self growth?
  • Can I request a personalized poem or leave feedback?

Color Scheme

  • #787775 – Primary text and headings
  • #97978f – Secondary text and navigation
  • #c4b3a9 – Accent color for buttons and highlights
  • #e1e2e6 – Section backgrounds
  • #ebebeb – Page background

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Headings and poem titles
  • Inter – Body text and navigation

Wireframes

Wireframes include a simple header area, navigation below, vertically stacked content sections, and a footer with references.
